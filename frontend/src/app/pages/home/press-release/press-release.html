Más Latino Expands into Women's Soccer Through Partnership with Miami Grove FC

Elevating Emerging Talent and Growing the Game Ahead of World Cup Momentum

Miami Grove FC — UPSL Women's Spring Season

Miami, FL — June 15, 2026 — Más Latino, a rapidly growing Spanish-language media company reaching over 100 million impressions, today announced a strategic partnership with Miami Grove FC for the upcoming UPSL Women's Spring Season, expanding its presence in women's sports and supporting the next generation of talent.

As part of this partnership, Más Latino will amplify Miami Grove FC's season through its multi-platform media ecosystem, powered by one of the leading Spanish-language podcast networks in the U.S. and a high-reach digital distribution engine across TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Facebook, and its mobile app. Through this ecosystem, Más Latino will deliver culturally relevant content, real-time coverage, and storytelling that connects fans directly to the team, while positioning Miami as a key destination for Latino audiences leading into the 2026 FIFA World Cup.

“There's real talent coming out of Miami, and it deserves to be seen at scale,” said Luis Morales Jr., CEO of Más Latino. “We're building the platform that makes that happen.”

Morales added, “What excites me most is being close to the grassroots where the talent is built and the culture lives. Miami has that energy, and we're proud to be part of it as it grows.”

Miami Grove FC, based in Miami, Florida, is a semi-professional women's soccer club founded in 2024 with a mission to provide a highly competitive and professional environment for players to develop, perform, and gain visibility. Recognized for its "Pathway to the Pros" philosophy, the club creates meaningful opportunities for athletes to advance into professional and international play.

“Our vision has always been to create real pathways for players to reach the next level, and partnerships like this help make that possible,” said Manuel Arias, CEO of Miami Grove FC. “Más Latino brings not only visibility, but a platform that understands our community and the importance of telling these stories the right way. This is a big step forward for our players and for women's soccer in Miami.”

This partnership reflects Más Latino's continued expansion into sports, culture, and community-driven storytelling, particularly as momentum builds during the 2026 FIFA World Cup and increased demand for inclusive, culturally relevant sports coverage.

About Más Latino

Más Latino is a rapidly growing Spanish-language media company reaching over 100 million impressions, built to inform, engage, and uplift Latino communities across the United States. With a powerful presence on Más Latino 99.9FM & 1300AM, one of the leading Spanish-language podcast networks in the country, a high-reach digital ecosystem across TikTok, Instagram, YouTube and Facebook, live sports coverage, and a dedicated mobile app, Más Latino delivers culturally relevant, community-driven content that moves culture forward, connecting brands and partners directly to the heart of the Latino market.

About Miami Grove FC

Miami Grove FC is a semi-professional women's soccer club based in Miami, Florida, founded in 2024. Guided by its "Pathway to the Pros" philosophy, the club is committed to giving players a competitive, professional environment to develop, perform, and gain visibility on their way to professional and international play.

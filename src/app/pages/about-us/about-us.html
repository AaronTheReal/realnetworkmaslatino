About Us

Not just a media company — a cultural force.

Más Latino Network is the leading bilingual media platform amplifying Latino voices, culture, and community from the heart of Boston to audiences across the United States. We are proudly Latino-owned, rooted in authenticity, and driven by purpose.

Founded by Luis Morales Jr., a second-generation entrepreneur and community builder, Más Latino was created to disrupt the way media connects with one of the fastest-growing and most influential demographics in the U.S. — the Latino community.

Through a powerful ecosystem that includes FM/AM radio (Más Latino 99.9FM & 1300AM), original podcasts, viral video content, a custom mobile app, and live cultural events, we reach millions of bilingual and bicultural Latinos who are shaping the future of America.

We’re not just storytellers — we’re community architects, culture translators, and brand partners.

Whether we’re interviewing politicians, livestreaming soccer matches, or hosting conversations about business, family, and faith — everything we create is rooted in representation, connection, and elevation.

Más Latino is where influence meets identity — and where brands, agencies, and institutions come to build meaningful relationships with Latino audiences.
